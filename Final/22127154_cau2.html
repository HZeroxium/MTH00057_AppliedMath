3. tng thc hin

Trong bi ton ny, ta s s dng xch Markov m hnh ha qu trnh xung cp v CSVC ca cc phng hc thuc ta nh I. Gi $X_t$ l bin ngu nhin m t trng thi ca CSVC ca cc phng hc vo nm th $t$. Ta c th m t xch Markov ny bng ma trn chuyn trng thi $P$ v vector phn phi xc sut ban u $\pi_0$. Tp trng thi ca xch Markov l $S = \{1, 2, 3, 4\}$ tng ng vi cc trng thi cht lng CSVC ca cc phng hc.

a. Xy dng ma trn chuyn trng thi $P$

Ma trn chuyn trng thi $P$ l ma trn vi $P_{ij}$ l xc sut chuyn t mt trng thi $j$ sang mt trng thi $i$ trong mt bc thi gian. Ta c th xy dng ma trn $P$ t bng s liu cho trc nh sau bng 2 cch suy lun ngc v suy lun tin:

  • Suy lun ngc: Bt u t trng thi $4$ (CSVC xu), ta thy nm 2020 c 1 phng c trng thi $4$, tuy nhin n nm $2021$ th c 2 phng. Suy ra c 1 phng ca nm 2020 chuyn t trng thi $3$ sang trng thi $4$. T suy ra trong 3 phng trng thi $3$ ca nm 2020, ch c 2 phng gi nguyn trang thi $3$ vo nm 2021, nhng s lng phng trng thi $3$ vo nm 2021 l 5, suy ra c 3 phng nm 2020 chuyn t trng thi $2$ sang trng thi $3$. Tng t, ta suy lun ra cc buc chuyn trng thi khc.
  • SUy lun tin: Bt u t trng thi $1$ (CSVC tt), ta thy nm 2020 c 34 phng c trng thi $1$, tuy nhin n nm $2021$ th s phng cn li l 26. Suy ra c 8 phng chuyn t trng thi $1$ sang trng thi $2$. V s phng trng thi $2$ ca nm 2021 ch l 17 nn c $8 + 12 - 17 = 3$ phng chuyn t trng thi $2$ sang trng thi $3$. Tng t, ta suy lun ra cc bc chuyn trng thi khc.

tm tt, ta c bng s liu suy lun sau:

Trng thi Ban u (2020) chuyn Gi nguyn Lc sau (2021)
1 34 8 26 26
2 12 3 9 17
3 3 1 2 5
4 1 0 1 2

T bng s liu trn, ta c ma trn chuyn trng thi $P$ nh sau:

$$ P = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{26}{34} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ \frac{8}{34} & \frac{9}{12} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{3}{12} & \frac{2}{3} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{3} & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$

b. Xc nh vector phn phi xc sut ban u $\pi_0$

Vector phn phi xc sut ban u $\pi_0$ l vector xc sut ca trng thi ca xch Markov vo thi im ban u. Ta c th xc nh vector $\pi_0$ t bng s liu cho trc nh sau: (s lng phng trng thi $i$ ca nm 2021)/(tng s phng) vi $i = 1, 2, 3, 4$.

$$ \pi_0 = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{26}{50} & \frac{17}{50} & \frac{5}{50} & \frac{2}{50} \end{bmatrix}^T $$

c. D bo s xung cp v CSVC ca cc phng hc trong vng 15 nm ti

d bo s xung cp v CSVC ca cc phng hc trong vng 15 nm ti, ta s s dng ma trn chuyn trng thi $P$ v vector phn phi xc sut ban u $\pi_0$ xc nh trn. Ta s tnh vector phn phi xc sut ca trng thi ca xch Markov vo cc nm tip theo bng cch ln lt nhn ma trn chuyn trng thi $P$ vi vector phn phi xc sut ca trng thi ca xch Markov nm trc .

V d: xc nh vector phn phi xc sut ca trng thi ca xch Markov vo nm 2022, ta s tnh $$\pi_1 = P \cdot \pi_0$$ Tng t, xc nh vector phn phi xc sut ca trng thi ca xch Markov vo nm 2023, ta s tnh $$\pi_2 = P \cdot \pi_1 = P^2 \cdot \pi_0$$ Tng t, vi nm 2035, ta s tnh $$\pi_{14} = P^{14} \cdot \pi_0$$.

d. Xc nh thi im (nm) phi ph hy xy li/trng tu ta nh I

xc nh thi im (nm) phi ph hy xy li/trng tu ta nh I, ta s xc nh nm m tt c cc phng hc u trng thi CSVC xu (trng thi $4$). lm iu ny, ta s tnh s nm $t$ sao cho phn t th $4$ ca vector phn phi xc sut ca trng thi ca xch Markov vo nm $t$ l $1$. $$ \pi_t = P^t \cdot \pi_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}^T $$

Ta cng d dng nhn thy rng nu tt c cc phng hc u trng thi CSVC xu, th sau tt c cc phng hc s vn trng thi CSVC xu. V vy, $\pi_{t+1} = P \cdot \pi_t = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}^T$ nn $\pi_t$ cng chnh l phn phi dng ca xch Markov.

e. Xc nh tui th ca ta nh I

Tui th ca ta nh I chnh l s nm m tt c cc phng hc u trng thi CSVC xu. T kt qu cu d, ta s xc nh tui th ca ta nh I bng cch ly s nm $t$ tm c tr i nm 2021.

4.2. Xy dng ma trn chuyn trng thi $P$ v vector phn phi xc sut ban u $\pi_0$

def calculate_transition_matrix_and_initial_distribution(
    data: Matrix,
) -> tuple[Matrix, Matrix, int]:
    """
    Tnh ton ma trn chuyn trng thi v vector phn phi xc sut ban u.

    Args:
        data (Matrix): Ma trn d liu u vo, mi hng i din cho mt trng thi vi
                       s lng phng hc  hai thi im khc nhau.

    Returns:
        tuple[Matrix, Matrix, int]: Tr v ma trn chuyn trng thi P, vector phn phi xc sut ban u pi_0,
                                    v tng s phng hc.
    """
    num_states = data.rows

    # Khi to ma trn P vi cc gi tr bng 0
    P_data = [[0] * num_states for _ in range(num_states)]

    # Tnh ton xc sut chuyn trng thi cho mi trng thi
    for i in range(num_states):
        if i == 0:
            P_data[i][i] = data[i][1] / data[i][0]  # Gi nguyn trng thi u tin
            if num_states > 1:
                P_data[i + 1][i] = (data[i][0] - data[i][1]) / data[i][
                    0
                ]  # Chuyn t trng thi u tin sang trng thi tip theo
        elif i < num_states - 1:
            rooms_down = sum([data[j][0] - data[j][1] for j in range(i)]) + (
                data[i][0] - data[i][1]
            )
            P_data[i][i] = (data[i][0] - rooms_down) / data[i][
                0
            ]  # Gi nguyn trng thi hin ti
            P_data[i + 1][i] = (
                rooms_down / data[i][0]
            )  # Chuyn xung trng thi tip theo
        else:
            P_data[i][i] = 1  # Trng thi cui cng, ch c th gi nguyn

    P = Matrix(P_data)

    # Tnh vector phn phi xc sut ban u pi_0
    total_rooms = sum([data[i][1] for i in range(num_states)])
    pi_0_data = [[data[i][1] / total_rooms] for i in range(num_states)]
    pi_0 = Matrix(pi_0_data)

    return P, pi_0, total_rooms

4.3. D bo s xung cp v CSVC ca cc phng hc trong vng 15 nm ti

Trong phn ny, em s d bo s xung cp v CSVC ca cc phng hc trong vng 15 nm ti t ma trn chuyn trng thi $P$ v vector phn phi xc sut ban u $\pi_0$. Chi tit cch d bo c trnh by phn 3.

  • Trong biu din s lng phng tng trng thi. m bo rng tng s phng hc d on trong tng nm vn l s nguyn v khng vt qu hoc thp hn tng s phng (total_rooms), chng ta c th s dng mt thut ton lm trn c kim sot. Mt trong nhng cch ph bin lm iu ny l s dng thut ton lm trn vi trng s (Weighted Rounding Algorithm), c th l phng php Largest Remainder Method (phng php phn d ln nht). Tuy nhin, d khng gy ra sai s tng s phng hc, phng php ny c th to ra sai s s lng phng hc tng trng thi, iu ny em tm thi chp nhn n gin ha bi ton v tp trung vo mc tiu chnh l d bo s xung cp v CSVC ca cc phng hc.
  • tng ca phng php:

    • Lm trn xung cho tt c cc gi tr.

def forecast_degradation(P: Matrix, pi_0: Matrix, years: int = 15) -> pd.DataFrame:
    """
    D bo s xung cp ca CSVC trong vng 'years' nm ti.

    Args:
        P (Matrix): Ma trn chuyn trng thi.
        pi_0 (Matrix): Vector phn phi xc sut ban u.
        years (int): S nm  d bo (mc nh l 15).

    Returns:
        pd.DataFrame: Bng cha phn phi xc sut cho tng nm.
    """
    forecast = []
    pi_t = pi_0
    for t in range(years + 1):  # Bao gm nm hin ti
        forecast.append([round(x[0], 4) for x in pi_t.data])
        pi_t = P * pi_t

    # To DataFrame  hin th kt qu
    years_list = [init_year + i for i in range(years + 1)]
    df_forecast = pd.DataFrame(
        forecast, columns=[f"Trng thi {i+1}" for i in range(P.rows)], index=years_list
    )

    return df_forecast


def forecast_degradation_integer(
    P: Matrix, pi_0: Matrix, total_rooms: int, years: int = 15
) -> pd.DataFrame:
    """
    D bo s xung cp ca CSVC trong vng 'years' nm ti vi d liu  dng s nguyn.

    Args:
        P (Matrix): Ma trn chuyn trng thi.
        pi_0 (Matrix): Vector phn phi xc sut ban u.
        total_rooms (int): Tng s phng hc.
        years (int): S nm  d bo (mc nh l 15).

    Returns:
        pd.DataFrame: Bng cha phn phi xc sut cho tng nm di dng s nguyn.
    """
    forecast = []
    pi_t = pi_0

    for t in range(years + 1):  # Bao gm nm hin ti
        # Nhn phn phi xc sut vi tng s phng hc  c s phng d on
        raw_forecast = [x[0] * total_rooms for x in pi_t.data]

        # Lm trn xung v tnh phn d
        rounded_forecast = [int(x) for x in raw_forecast]
        remainders = [x - r for x, r in zip(raw_forecast, rounded_forecast)]

        # Tnh tng sau khi lm trn
        total_after_rounding = sum(rounded_forecast)

        # Phn b phn d cho cc trng thi c phn d ln nht
        while total_after_rounding < total_rooms:
            max_index = remainders.index(max(remainders))
            rounded_forecast[max_index] += 1
            remainders[max_index] = 0  # t phn d v 0  khng phn b thm na
            total_after_rounding += 1

        forecast.append(rounded_forecast)
        pi_t = P * pi_t

    # To DataFrame  hin th kt qu
    years_list = [init_year + i for i in range(years + 1)]
    df_forecast = pd.DataFrame(
        forecast, columns=[f"Trng thi {i+1}" for i in range(P.rows)], index=years_list
    )

    return df_forecast

def find_rebuild_year(
    P: Matrix,
    pi_0: Matrix,
    total_rooms: int,
    init_year: int = 2021,
    precision: int = 4,
) -> int:
    """
    Xc nh thi im cn ph hy/trng tu ta nh khi tt c phng hc u  trng thi xu.

    Args:
        P (Matrix): Ma trn chuyn trng thi.
        pi_0 (Matrix): Vector phn phi xc sut ban u.
        total_rooms (int): Tng s phng hc.
        start_year (int): Nm bt u theo di (mc nh l 2021).
        precision (int): S ch s thp phn  so snh (mc nh l 4).

    Returns:
        int: Nm cn ph hy/trng tu.
    """
    pi_t = pi_0
    year = init_year
    while True:
        if (
            round(pi_t[-1][0] * total_rooms, ndigits=precision) == total_rooms
        ):  # Tt c phng hc u  trng thi 4
            return year
        pi_t = P * pi_t
        year += 1


# S dng hm  xc nh nm cn ph hy/trng tu
rebuild_year = find_rebuild_year(
    P, pi_0, total_rooms, init_year=init_year, precision=precision
)
print(f"d. Nm cn ph hy/trng tu ta nh: {rebuild_year}")


def calculate_lifetime(rebuild_year: int, start_year: int = 2000) -> int:
    """
    Xc nh tui th ca ta nh I.

    Args:
        rebuild_year (int): Nm ph hy/trng tu.
        start_year (int): Nm bt u theo di (mc nh l 2021).

    Returns:
        int: Tui th ca ta nh I.
    """
    return rebuild_year - start_year
